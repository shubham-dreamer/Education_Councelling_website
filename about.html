About Us

Counselling ADDA has strong collaborative links with universities, colleges and schools in the UK, mainland Europe, Australia, USA and Canada which enable all students' study needs to be met. The available courses are from business and computing through the full range of university disciplines to medicine, dentistry and health studies. These university are based on a high degree of trust and in many cases the universities are able to guarantee a place to students who have completed the GEC in-country programmes at either Foundation, Certificate or Diploma level.
EDUCATIONAL CONSULTANCY This division of Counselling ADDA provides advice and support with respect to all aspects of setting up and managing educational institutions or departments. This includes the design of organisations for delivering educational programmes, the development of existing organisations, curriculum development and the setting up and managing of franchise arrangements, or other forms of collaboration, between universities and colleges in the UK and overseas.
TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT This division of Counselling ADDA provides training and management development programmes for all branches of industry and commerce. These programmes are tailor-made to suit the requirements of the particular organisation. They usually take the form of short courses on specific topics, but may be linked to academic programmes such as an MBA through credit arrangements.
Counselling ADDA training services are offered to industry, commerce, government and local government agencies and educational institutions.
